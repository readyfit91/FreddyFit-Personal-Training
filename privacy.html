Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Information We Collect

When you submit the consultation request form at getfreddyfit.com, or a lead inquiry form connected to our CRM at myfitpro.vercel.app, we collect the information you provide — which may include your name, phone number, email address, fitness goals, training preferences, current weight, and any medical history or injuries you choose to share. If you become a client, we also maintain records related to your training program, session scheduling, and progress tracking through our CRM at myfitpro.vercel.app.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to respond to your consultation request, schedule and confirm training sessions, communicate with you about your fitness program, and provide coaching services. This includes contacting you by email, phone call, or SMS text message.

Text Messaging (SMS)

If you provide your phone number, FreddyFit may send you text messages related to your inquiry or your training sessions — such as consultation follow-up, appointment confirmations, and scheduling reminders sent through our CRM. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on your interactions with us. You can opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP or contact us at the email below.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties for marketing purposes. Your information is only shared with the service providers we rely on to operate our business (such as our scheduling CRM, database, email, and payment processors), solely to the extent necessary to provide those services.

Payments

Package payments are processed through Stripe, a third-party payment processor. We do not store your payment card details on our servers.

Data Retention

We retain client and lead information for as long as needed to provide our services and maintain business records, or until you request deletion.

Your Choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us using the information below.

Contact Us

Questions about this policy or your information can be sent to myfitpro@getfreddyfit.com.
