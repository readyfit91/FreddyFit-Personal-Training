PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: July 17, 2026

FreddyFit LLC ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains how FreddyFit LLC collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website, request a consultation, communicate with us, or use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Fitness goals and consultation information
  • Communication preferences

How We Use Information

FreddyFit LLC uses your information to:

  • Respond to consultation requests
  • Schedule appointments
  • Provide fitness coaching services
  • Communicate regarding services and customer support
  • Improve our website and services

SMS/Text Messaging Privacy

By providing your mobile phone number and opting into SMS messages, you consent to receive text messages from FreddyFit LLC related to consultations, appointments, coaching updates, and customer support.

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. You may reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe or HELP for assistance.

Mobile information and SMS consent will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties.

Information Sharing

FreddyFit LLC does not sell your personal information.

Information may only be shared when necessary to provide requested services, comply with legal obligations, or protect our rights.

Cookies and Website Analytics

FreddyFit LLC may use cookies and analytics tools to improve website functionality and understand visitor interactions.

Data Security

FreddyFit LLC takes reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

Your Choices

You may request updates, corrections, or deletion of your personal information by contacting FreddyFit LLC.

Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact FreddyFit LLC through the contact information provided on this website.
