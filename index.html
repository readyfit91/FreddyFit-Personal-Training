GET STARTED WITH FREDDYFIT

Your journey to fitness excellence begins with our proven FunctionalFIT Package

The Foundation

FunctionalFIT Package

Whether you choose to train at home, in our Oakville studio, online, or in a small group, every client must complete their individual FunctionalFIT Package. This program includes 4 one-on-one sessions spread over 3–4 weeks, designed to assess your body, establish a baseline, and set you up for long-term success.

$299.00 Get Started Now

What to Expect

CONSULTATION

Initial Assessment & Goal Setting

  • Discuss your goals using the SMART approach (Specific, Measurable, Attainable, Realistic, and Timely) for both short-term and long-term objectives.
  • Identify any mental and physical obstacles that may impact your training progress.
  • Record your body weight and body fat metrics to establish a clear baseline.
  • Build a strong foundation for your personalized fitness journey.
Schedule Your Complimentary Consultation

Your Program

YOUR 4-SESSION JOURNEY

01

Session 1

Lower Body Functional Screening

  • Identify current movement patterns and any potential limitations.
  • Conduct a functional screening assessment of the lower body.
  • Perform supporting lower-body breakout assessments as needed (ankle, knee, hip, and Lumbo-Pelvic-Hip Complex).
  • Assign food logging to track nutrition habits and daily intake.
  • Assign one individualized program to complete.
02

Session 2

Corrective Protocol & HIIT Workout

  • Incorporate and assign a daily lower-body corrective protocol based on findings from Session 1.
  • Review Phase 1 of your personalized nutrition plan.
  • Complete a HIIT workout to assess cardiovascular fitness.
  • Assign two individualized programs to complete between sessions.
03

Session 3

Upper Body Screening & Assessments

  • Conduct a functional screening assessment of the upper body.
  • Perform supporting upper-body breakout assessments as needed (shoulder and neck).
  • Assign two individualized programs to complete between sessions.
  • Discuss the projected length of training required to achieve your goals.
  • Provide a customized email outline detailing the recommended training investment and estimated timeframe.
04

Session 4

Programming Foundation & Strength Testing

  • Incorporate and assign a daily upper-body corrective protocol based on findings from Session 3.
  • Break down one new barbell functional movement with proper technique and coaching cues.
  • Work up to a 5-rep max on the functional movement to establish a benchmark.
  • Discuss scheduling options should you choose to continue training.

Between Sessions

After each session, you'll be assigned tasks—whether corrective exercises, nutrition adjustments, or movement-based activities—to reinforce your training and accelerate progress.

✓

Our Commitment to You

We stand behind the quality of our FunctionalFIT Package. If at any point during your initial 4 sessions you feel this program isn't the right fit for you, we offer a 100% money-back guarantee. All we ask is that you provide genuine feedback on what we can do to improve. Your success and satisfaction are our top priorities.

Continue Your Journey

WHAT HAPPENS NEXT?

Upon completing the FunctionalFIT Package, we integrate your goals with the data collected to create a personalized, long-term training plan. Based on your preferred approach, we transition you into the training style that fits your lifestyle.

FREDDY

NASM-CPT · CNC · CES · ETA Certified

Freddy is a NASM-Certified Personal Trainer (CPT), Certified Nutrition Coach (CNC), and Corrective Exercise Specialist (CES) serving clients in Saint Louis, Missouri with a lifelong commitment to results-driven, client-focused coaching.

He holds dual degrees (Bachelor's and Master's in Public Accounting) from the University of Missouri–St. Louis, bringing a structured, analytical approach to training, program design, and measurable results.

Freddy leads by example. He has competed in Powerlifting and Mixed Martial Arts, holding the state deadlift record at 711 lbs from 2015–2022, and earning a Mixed Martial Arts Amateur Title with Shamrock Promotions in 2018. He believes the trainer you hire should live the standards they teach — both inside and outside the gym.

His philosophy emphasizes comprehensive movement assessment and corrective exercise, helping clients rebuild movement quality, prevent injury, and achieve long-term results. His ETA certifications position him at the forefront of exercise therapy and corrective coaching in the Midwest.

Our Mission

SUSTAINABLE RESULTS THROUGH SMARTER TRAINING

Our mission is to create sustainable results by meeting clients at their point of struggle and building systems that support long-term physical, mental, and emotional well-being.

Unlike generic workout programs, FreddyFit focuses on individualized personal training built around how your body actually moves — not just what exercises you perform.

Whether your goal is weight loss in Saint Louis, strength development, injury prevention, or athletic performance, our approach is designed to support you safely, efficiently, and long term.

Our focus isn't just on training harder — it's on training smarter, rebuilding movement quality, and creating a strong foundation for sustainable results.

Our Approach

COMPREHENSIVE MOVEMENT ASSESSMENT

Every client undergoes a comprehensive movement assessment evaluating mechanics at every major joint — starting at the feet and progressing upward through the ankles, knees, hips, spine, shoulders, and neck.

01
Pain Patterns
Identify pain patterns and movement limitations that may be holding you back from performing at your best.
02
Injury Risks
Increase awareness of potential injury risks before they become problems, keeping you safe and progressing.
03
Imbalances
Detect muscular imbalances, compensations, and mobility restrictions affecting your movement quality.
04
Safe Training
Determine how to train you safely and effectively with customized strategies that rebuild proper movement.
